Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LocalForge

Is LocalForge really free?

Yes! LocalForge is completely free and open source. No hidden costs, no premium tiers, no subscriptions. Use it for personal or commercial projects without any restrictions.

What operating systems are supported?

Currently, LocalForge is available for Linux (Ubuntu, Debian, and derivatives). Windows and macOS support is planned for future releases.

Do I need Docker or containers?

No! LocalForge installs development tools directly on your system. This provides better performance and easier debugging compared to containerized solutions.

Can I manage existing projects?

Absolutely! While LocalForge can create new projects, you can also use it to configure Nginx virtual hosts and SSL certificates for your existing projects.

How do I get support?

Check our comprehensive documentation, join discussions on GitHub, or report issues in our repository. Our community is here to help!
